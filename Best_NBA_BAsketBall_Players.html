    James Harden

    James Harden from WIkimedia Commons. CC-BY

    James Edward Harden Jr. is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Houston Rockets of the NBA. He was selected by the Oklahoma City Thunder with 3rd overall pick in the 2007 NBA Draft. He played two seasons of college basketball with the Arizona State Sun Devils in Tempe, Arizona. He has been selected to the NBA All-Star game seven times, has been a five time All-NBA First Team Selection, two time NBA Scoring champion, and NBA Most Valuable Player in 2018. He can play both the shooting guard and point guard position. He is 6 feet and 5 inches tall and weighs about 220 pounds. He started his career off with the Oklahaoma City Thunder and played with the for three seasons. After failing to reach a contract agreement with the Oklahoma City Thunder, he was traded to the Houston Rockets before the 2012 - 2013 NBA season. He is known as one of the best NBA shooting guard and is known for his top two unique moves

    Paul George

    Paul George from Wikimedia Commons. CC-BY

    Paul Cliftonantho George is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Los Angeles Clippers of the NBA. He was selected by the Indiana Pacers with the 10th overall pick in the 2010 NBA Draft. He played two seasons of college basketball with the Calfornia State University(Fresno State) in Fresno, California. He has been selected to the NBA All-Star game six times, has been a five time All-NBA Team Selction, and has been a four time member of the NBA All-Defensive Team. He can play both the shooting guard and small forward position due to his size. He is 6 feet and 8 inches tall and weighs about 220 pounds. He started his career off with the Indian Pacers and played for 7 years with them. The following season, he was traded to the Oklahoma City Thunder and played for two seasons. Before the 2019-2020 season started, he was traded to the Los Angeles Clippers. He is also known as PG-13 and later changed his jersey number from 24 to 13.

    Stephen Curry

    Stephen Curry from Wikimedia Commons. CC-BY

    Wardell Stephen Curry II is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Golden State Warriors of the NBA. He was selected by the Golden State Warriors with 7th overall pick in the 2009 NBA Draft. He played three seasons of college basketball with the Davidson Wildcats in Davidson, North Carolina. He has been selected to the NBA All-Star game six time, has been nominated twice for NBA Most Valuable Player, has been a three time All-NBA First Team selection, and has won three NBA championships with the Golden State Warriors. He plays the point guard position and has been known as the greatest shooter of all time. He has also been known to utlize the three point shot along with his teammates Kevin Durant, Klay Thompson, and Draymond Green. He is 6 feet and 3 inches tall and weighs about 185 pounds. He has so far played every season with the team that drafted him, Golden State Warriors. He is the son of former NBA player, Dell Curry, and is also the older brother of Seth Curry, who is also an NBA player who currently plays for the Dallas Mavericks. He is currently in third place for most all-time NBA three point shots made and also formed a duo with his teamate, Klay Thompson, which was nicknamed the Splash Brothers.

    D'Angelo Russell

    D'Angelo Russell from Wikimedia Commons. CC-BY

    D'Angelo Dante Russell is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Minnesota Timberwolve of the NBA. He was selected by the Los Angeles Lakers with the 2nd overall pick in the 2015 NBA Draft. He played one season of college basketball with the Ohio State Buckeyes in Columbus, Ohio. He has been selected to the NBA All-Star game one time, was named to the NBA All-Rookie Second Team with the Lakers in 2015, and was selected as McDonalds All American in 2014. He plays the point guard position and shooting guard position. He has also been known as, DLo, which is his nickname. He is 6 feet and 4 inches tall and weighs about 193 pounds. He started his career off with the Los Angeles Lakers and played with them for two seasosn. He was then shortly traded to the Brooklyn Nets which is where he became famous for leading them into the playoffs after a long, long time. On July 7, 2019, he was sent to the Golden State Warriors as part of a sign-and-trade deal in for Kevin Durant who went to the Nets from the Golden State Warriors. On November 8, 2019, he scored a career high 52 points in 37 shots in a 125-119 overtime loss to the Minnesota Timberwolves.

    LeBron James

    LeBron James from Wikimedia Commons. CC-BY

    LeBron Raymone James Sr. is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Los Angeles Lakers of the NBA. He was selected by the Cleveland Cavaliers with the 1st overall pick in the 2003 NBA Draft. He did not play college basketball as he came straight out of his high school, St. Vincent-St. Mary. St. Vincent-St. Mary is a private Catholic high school in Akron, Ohio which won three divison titles in four years under the help of LeBron James. He has won three NBA Championships, has been a three time NBA Finals MVP, four time NBA Most Valuable Player, and has been a 15 time NBA All-Star selection. He has also been selected to the All-NBA First team twelve times. He was born to Gloria Marie James who at the time of his birth was 16 years old. His father, Anthony McClelland, did not play a role in his life who had a long criminal history. LeBron's life was a struggle as they moved from apartment to apartment as his mother struggled to find a job that would be best for the both of them early in his life. She let him move in with his football coach, Frank Walker, who introduced LeBron to basketball at the age of nine. His four friends, Sian Cotton, Dru Joyce III, and Willie McGee decided to attend the same high school together. They also nicknamed themselves the "Fab Four". He is 6 feet 9 inches tall and weighs about 250 pounds. He is considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time.
