Today's Selected Special Player

Kevin Durant

Kevin Durant from Wikimedia Commons. CC-BY

Kevin Durant, also known as KD, is an American Basketball Proffessional Player who currently plays for the Brookyln Nets. He was drafted by the Seattle Supersonics with the second overall pick in the 2007 NBA Draft. He played one year of college basketball with the Universiry of Texas Longhorns. So far in his career, he has won two NBA championships, an NBA MVP Award, two Finals MVP Awards, two NBA All-Star Game MVP Awards, four NBA scoring titles, the NBA Rookie Of The Year Award, and two Olympic gold medals. He has been selected to nine All-NBA teams and ten NBA All-Star teams. He also has a partnership with Nike and has realeased his own brand. He even has his signature shoes with the latest ones, KD12. He was unusually tall at a very young age (6 feet tall between ages 13 and 14) and has a height of 6 feet and 10 inches.
